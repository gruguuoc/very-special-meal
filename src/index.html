RAMEN

The taste of Japan

Ramen is a beloved Japanese dish that blends rich flavors, tradition, and culinary craftsmanship. More than just a meal, it's a cultural icon that represents Japan's deep-rooted love for food and innovation.

Main ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chicken or pork broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Scallions
  • Soft-boiled eggs
  • Cooked pork
  • Nori
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Sesame seeds

How to prepare the recipe

  1. Prepare the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pot, heat the chicken or pork broth and add soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  3. While the broth is simmering, prepare the toppings: slice the cooked pork or chicken, chop the scallions, and prepare soft-boiled eggs.
  4. Once the noodles are ready, drain them and place them in a bowl.
  5. Pour the hot broth over the noodles in the bowl.
  6. Add the toppings: place slices of pork or chicken, soft-boiled eggs, chopped scallions, and nori on top of the ramen.
  7. Sprinkle sesame seeds for extra flavor and garnish.
  8. Serve the ramen hot and enjoy!
